Rebuilds

When it comes to tuning I will give your piano the time and attention it needs to achieve the best possible result, be it a Victorian wooden framed instrument or a full size grand in a concert hall.
I therefore limit my appointments mostly to just three a day spending between one and two hours on average.
My restoration and piano manufacturing qualifications mean I am familiar with all aspects of repair and regulation and I carry the specialist tools and materials for most jobs with me on the road.
I also offer friendly advice on how best to care for your instrument, its value for insurance purposes and help with buying, selling and even transporting your piano.
