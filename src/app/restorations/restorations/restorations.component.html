Restorations

Contemporary Restorations

Restoring pianos is my true passion.
The workshop is set up on two floors to accommodate the three main aspects of contemporary piano restoration: Action and keyboard rebuilds; Strungback refurbishment (strings, frame and soundboard) and Cabinet restoration from joinery and veneering to hand french-polishing in collaboration with (link the finishing room?).
I work to a very high standard and am pleased to offer a ten year guarantee with all rebuilds.

Period Restorations

Over the last few decades the rules and methods of period-piece restorations have changed no end for the better.
More and more information has been documented over the years making research a large and crucial part of any project.
Through a specialist network of revered instrument makers and technicians I am able to participate in the ongoing debates surrounding best practice, source the best available materials and access technical drawings and information essential for an authentic end result.
If you have a stringed keyboard instrument older than 175 years that requires major work please contact me to arrange a consultation.
